About Us

Trigger solutions is a leading provider of Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software solutions, offering integrated solutions to manage the business operations of other companies. These solutions include various functional modules such as human resources management, accounting, inventory management, customer relationship management, and many other units that help companies improve their efficiency and increase their profits. This company is distinguished by the high quality of its solutions, which are flexible and adaptable to the different needs of companies, thereby efficiently meeting the needs of its customers.

Why ERPNext Software?

ERPNext is your Perfect choice to run your company! ERPNext is an open-source enterprise resource planning software that assists businesses in daily operations such as accounting, procurement, inventory management, project management, human resources, and customer relationship management. It is based on a modular architecture, which enables businesses to tailor the software to their specific requirements.

    Provide businesses with a wide range of features to help them manage their day-to-day operations.

  • 02 User Friendly Interface

  • 03 Cloud-Based

    Ability to access data anywhere and at any time with the flexibility to work remotely or on the go.

 

Services

ERPNext is a full featured business management solution with 1000+ objects to help you run your business

Accounting

Manage the whole subscription billing lifecycle for your customers. With payment requests and alerts through email or SMS reminders, you can effortlessly invoice your customers and pursue receivable payments.

Inventory

Intelligently track order fulfillment, eliminate improper inventory purchases, regulate stock levels, implement promotional schemes, and streamline your distribution operations.

Sales & Purchase

Track inventory levels, restock stock, and manage sales orders, customers, suppliers, shipments, deliverables, and order fulfillment.

Point of Sale (POS)

Measure, manage and sell more with a comprehensive web-based solution to help you streamline retail management, track inventory, manage customers, and increase business profitability.

Manufacturing

Simplifies the production cycle, assists in tracking material use, exhibits capacity planning, manages subcontracts, and more.

Human Resources (HR)

Maintains a comprehensive employee database that includes contact information, compensation information, attendance, performance evaluation, leaves, and appraisal records.

Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

Track business opportunities from leads and customers, send them quotations, and place sales orders.

Project Management

Effectively manage internal and external initiatives. Using the Time Sheets tool, bill your clients for the stuff you've deployed to their location. Create and manage tasks to keep your personnel alert throughout critical projects.
